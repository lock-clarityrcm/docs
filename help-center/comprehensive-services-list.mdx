<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Revenue Cycle Management">
    ### Full revenue cycle management (RCM)

    Clarity RCM manages the entire lifecycle of your insurance-based claims, known as Claim Bills (CBs), from the moment a provider finalizes their notes until the claim is fully resolved and settled.

    | Service | Description |
    |---------|-------------|
    | **Charge Entry & Claim Submission** | We post and submit all of your insurance claims. Your providers simply need to finalize their notes. |
    | **Claim Scrubbing & Edits** | We scrub all insurance claims before submission to ensure a high first-pass acceptance rate. |
    | **Denial & Rejection Management** | Our team works all denials, rejections, and invalid claims with expert certified coders. |
    | **Payment Posting** | We manage and post all insurance payments and patient payments related to insurance claims. |
    | **Appeals & Audits** | We handle all claim appeals and manage records requested for soft audits or internal payer reviews. |
    | **Patient Statements** | We manage and send patient statements for balances related to insurance claims. |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Operational Support">
    ### Comprehensive operational support

    | Service | Description |
