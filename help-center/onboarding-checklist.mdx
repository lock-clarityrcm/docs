This checklist outlines the initial steps needed to get our teams integrated and your account set up for success. Complete these items to ensure a smooth onboarding process.

<Note>

**Two Phases:** Phase 1 covers essential system access. Phase 2 sets up patient services integration. Complete all items in order for the smoothest onboarding.

</Note>

These first steps are crucial for us to begin the preliminary review of your account.

| 2 | Communicate start date | ☐ |
| 3 | Provide TriZetto login | ☐ |
| 4 | Provide TEP access | ☐ |
| 5 | Create Firm Admin login | ☐ |

## Phase 2: Patient services integration

These steps will help us take over patient billing inquiries.

| Step | Action | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| 6 | Create PayJunction login | ☐ |
| 7 | Create Klara login | ☐ |
| 8 | Decide on patient call routing | ☐ |

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 6: Create a PayJunction login">

  **Purpose:** Required for processing patient refunds and setting up payment plans.

  **Action:** Create a user login for `websites-southwest@clarityrcm.com`

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 7: Create a Klara login">

  **Purpose:** Allows the Clarity RCM team to monitor and respond to patient services questions.

  **Action:** Create a user login for `Klara-psconnect@clarityrcm.com`

  **Note:** Initially, messages may go to your current billing inbox. We will collaborate with you to determine the best way to handle patient communication going forward.

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Step 8: Decide on patient call routing">

  Choose one of the following options for directing patient billing calls to our Patient Services department:

  | Option | Description |
  |--------|-------------|
  | **Option A** | Connect your practice's phone tree directly to our Patient Services department |
  | **Option B** | Update your patient statements to list our direct line: **[502-480-1940](tel:502-480-1940)** |

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What happens next?

<Check>

**Once you complete these steps**, your dedicated Account Managers will:
1. Conduct a preliminary review of your account
2. Communicate the next steps
3. Send details about Clarity RCM's and your practice's responsibilities

</Check>

For any urgent matters, please email **[support@clarityrcm.com](mailto:support@clarityrcm.com)** in addition to your account managers.
