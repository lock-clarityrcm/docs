As your strategic operating partner, we provide a dedicated team to ensure your practice achieves financial success and operational efficiency. While specific individuals will be introduced to you, this guide outlines the key roles you'll interact with as we begin our partnership.

<Note>

**Your Primary Contact:** Your Account Manager is your go-to for all day-to-day needs. You'll be introduced to your specific team members during onboarding.

</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Your dedicated Account Manager">

  **Role:** Your primary liaison and strategic advisor.

  This dedicated expert will be your main point of contact for your day-to-day needs and strategic oversight. They are responsible for guiding you through the onboarding process and ensuring a smooth transition.

  **Responsibilities:**
  - Performing the initial review of your account once preliminary information is received
  - Communicating next steps during the onboarding process
  - Providing detailed information about working with Clarity RCM and outlining general responsibilities
  - Serving as your go-to resource for any questions or needs that arise

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The billing operations & coding team">

  **Role:** The engine behind your revenue cycle management.

  This is the core team that manages the entire lifecycle of your insurance claims, from submission to settlement. They combine deep industry expertise with a data-driven platform to maximize your revenue.

  **Responsibilities:**
  - Managing all aspects of your insurance claims (Claim Bills or CBs), from posting and submission to managing payments
  - Scrubbing claims for issues before submission and working all denials, rejections, and invalid claims
  - Providing expert certified coders to handle complex coding denials and offer education on billing trends
  - Building and managing your list of payers and managing claim edits and rules
  - Handling all appeals and records requested during payer reviews

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The Patient Services team">

  **Role:** Your professional and compassionate patient billing support center.

  Our dedicated Patient Services (PS) team acts as an extension of your office, handling patient billing inquiries that occur after a visit has concluded. This allows your staff to focus on patient care.

  **Contact:** Patients can call or text **[502-480-1940](tel:502-480-1940)** for billing questions.

  **Responsibilities:**
  - Answering patient calls and texts regarding their medical insurance bills
  - Explaining balances, helping patients read their Explanation of Benefits (EOB), and clarifying why a balance is their responsibility (e.g., deductible, coinsurance)
  - Taking patient payments over the phone and setting up short-term payment plans for those expressing financial hardship
  - Updating patient insurance information and rebilling claims when necessary
  - Managing patient billing questions received through platforms like Klara

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The urgent support team">

  **Role:** Your rapid-response team for urgent issues.

  For critical situations requiring immediate attention, you have a direct line to our support system. This ensures that urgent claim issues or time-sensitive changes are addressed promptly.

  **How to Engage:** For any urgent claim or situation, email **[support@clarityrcm.com](mailto:support@clarityrcm.com)** along with your Account Manager and the Director of Client Services to ensure the quickest action.

  <Danger>

  **For urgent issues, email ALL THREE:** Your Account Manager + Lindsay Ethredge ([lindsay@clarityrcm.com](mailto:lindsay@clarityrcm.com)) + [support@clarityrcm.com](mailto:support@clarityrcm.com)

  </Danger>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
